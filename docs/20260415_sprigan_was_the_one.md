# Sprigan was the one

2026-04-15

When i was a kid i watched some japanese anime on TV (probably rented VCD). 

The most memorable scene was a guy at airport customs, loads of weapons spread on the table, and the officer said "are you here to start a war?"

Something else about he killed his training officers during adolescence. I might have mixed this up. 

Just now i confirmed it was "Sprigan". 

In fact, i remmeber right next to the airport scene, there was a panorama over many buildings and my aunt commented that in a chinese anime the buildings would be simple matchboxes. That matched as well.

Long live my memories.
